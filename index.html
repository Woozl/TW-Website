We are the Techno Wolves...

Team 5518 (the Techno Wolves) is a second year FIRST Robotics Competition team based in Raleigh, North Carolina! We were generously given a work space on North Carolina State University campus called the Solar House! Our team is primarily based out of Wake STEM Early College High School at NC State.

What we do

We are a FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) team. Every year in early January, we are presented a new challenge by FIRST and the goal is to design and build a robot that will solve this challenge. Teams from all over the world compete at regional and national levels for a chance to win the championship. During the off season (March-December), we prepare for the build season (January-February) by recruiting and training new members and gathering support and sponsorship.
